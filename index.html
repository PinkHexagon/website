Socially-conscious video services

Producing engaging videos for third sector, charitable organisations and private clients through thoughtful and accessible storytelling.

Impactful videos with social justice at heart

PH Media is Amanda Craig, a Scottish Palestinian video editor who has been creating videos for third sector and private clients for over 10 years, specialising in producing visually engaging films & animations which puts service users, charities and their mission at the heart of stories. Using an intersectional approach, Amanda has also worked as a creative youth worker and community artist within communities, working with and for young people across Scotland.

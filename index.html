About PH Media

PH Media is Amanda Craig, a Scottish Palestinian video editor who has been creating videos for third sector and private clients for over 10 years, specialising in producing visually engaging videos which puts service users, charities and their mission at the heart of stories. Using an intersectional approach, Amanda has also worked as a creative youth worker and artist within communities, working with and for young people across Scotland.
